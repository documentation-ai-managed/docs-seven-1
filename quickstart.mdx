## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- No prior installation required—Seven runs entirely in the browser
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for Seven in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up your project space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name It" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like "My Project Docs" and select a starter template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Slug" icon="link">
    Set a custom URL slug, e.g., `my-project-docs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Save and publish—your space is now live at `https://docs.example.com/my-project-docs`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Initial Setup and Customization

Tailor your space to fit your needs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Theme" icon="palette">
    In the space settings, select a brand color like `#3B82F6`. Upload a logo and adjust typography.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Navigation" icon="menu">
    Add pages via the sidebar. Drag to reorder and set a homepage.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Permissions" icon="shield">
    Invite collaborators with roles: Viewer, Editor, or Admin. Use `YOUR_API_KEY` for integrations.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable custom domains in advanced settings for production use.
</Callout>
