description: Trouble editing a page? Try these steps.
---

## Symptoms

- The **Edit** button is disabled
- Changes won't save

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Make sure you are a collaborator or admin in the space settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh browser">
    Reload the page to update your session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Try clearing your browser cache and cookies.
  </Step>
</Steps>
