---

## Steps to Invite

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your space settings">
    Go to your space, click the gear icon in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Members'">
    Find the **Members** tab in the settings menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite">
    Click **Invite Member**, enter their email, and choose a role.
    They'll get an email with instructions to join.
  </Step>
</Steps>
