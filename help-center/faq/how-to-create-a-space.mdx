## Create a Space

1. Go to your seven dashboard
2. Click **"New Space"**
3. Enter a name and description
4. Optionally add your team members
5. Click **"Create"**

Your new documentation space appears in your project list.

## Tip

Spaces help organize docs for different teams or clients.
